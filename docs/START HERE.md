# Byzantine Doors — Project Overview

**byzantinedoors.com** — Anglo-Byzantine catechetical and parish resources for the Ukrainian Catholic Church.

---

## What is this?

Byzantine Doors is a website (and eventually an ecosystem of tools) that serves two purposes:

1. **Evangelization resources** — A beautiful, modern, accessible entry point for seekers (Protestants, atheists, the curious) to discover Eastern Christianity through the Ukrainian Catholic tradition.
2. **Parish life resources** — Tools and content to support priests, cantors, and parishioners in living a Byzantine liturgical life.

## Who is it for?

- **Primary audience:** Seekers who know little or nothing about Eastern Christianity
- **Secondary audience:** Poorly catechized Ukrainian Catholics who attend Liturgy but can't explain what's happening or why

## Theological baseline

**Christ Our Pascha (COP)** — the catechism of the Ukrainian Catholic Church — is the primary theological norm. Whatever COP says on a topic is the baseline position. All content should track COP paragraph references.

## Tech stack (recommended)

- **Frontend:** Astro (static site generation, islands architecture)
- **CMS:** Sanity (structured content, custom schemas)
- **Hosting:** Vercel or Netlify
- **Fonts:** Cormorant Garamond (display), EB Garamond (body), Inter (UI) — all from Google Fonts
